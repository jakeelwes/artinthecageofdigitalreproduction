Art in the Cage of Digital Reproduction

On 26th November 2024, a group of artists and hacktivists took action to re-open OpenAI’s technology to the public. They provided indirect access to their video generation model, Sora, via an open source front end on HuggingFace. Alongside this, we released a statement to denormalise the exploitation of artists for research and development, training data and public relations by “corporate AI overlords” and calling for artists thinking beyond the proprietary and the prompt box. The ‘Sora leak’ and accompanying open letter quickly went viral and got shut down in 3 hours with international press coverage. https://huggingface.co/spaces/PR-Puppets/PR-Puppet-Sora
